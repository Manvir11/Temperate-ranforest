1. What are some of the major impacts that humans had done to the biome temperate forests?

  • Deforestation

    • Deforestation is an effect of mostly logging and other humans activities, which affects many temperate rainforests. In Australia, less than 3 percent of the original temperate rainforest remains.

  • Habbit loss and fragmentation

    • Human activities contribute to habitat loss and fragmentation in the temperate rainforests. By losing their natural habitats, some species become threaten and can migrate to other areas, affecting the balance of local populations.

  • Pollution

    • The pollution of large urban centers has also affected the remaining temperate rainforests. Industrial pollution contaminates water sources, contributing to the ecological unbalance between species in the food chain.
